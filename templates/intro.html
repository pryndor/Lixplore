Welcome to Lixplore

Lixplore is an open-source literature search and review tool for researchers and professionals. It connects to multiple sources like PubMed, CrossRef, Springer, Europe PMC, ClinicalTrials.gov, and more.

How to Use

  1. Select a data source from the dropdown (e.g., PubMed, CrossRef).
  2. Enter your search keyword, DOI, author name, or topic.
  3. Optionally filter by status or date range.
  4. Click Search to view results with abstracts, DOIs, and citation formats.
  5. Access your previous searches via the History page.
